Install, activate and get creating with Unity

Installing Unity should take just a few minutes. After you press “Continue” below, you will be prompted to accept the software license agreement. Then, you will be guided through choosing a destination on your computer for installing Unity. 

Once Unity is installed, you can then complete some last easy steps to activate your license. Thanks for downloading, and all the best creating with Unity.
